Privacy Policy
Last updated: July 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Yorha, operated by Yorha ("we," "us," "our"), collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Platform.

1. Information We Collect

CategoryExamples
Account informationName, email address, hashed password, role (reader/author), pen name and bio (authors)
Profile mediaAvatar and cover images, stored via Cloudinary
Transaction dataPurchase history, order records, author balance and earnings — processed via Paystack
Uploaded contentFiles uploaded by Authors (PDFs, ZIPs), stored via Backblaze B2
Technical dataIP address, device/browser type, and usage logs, collected automatically for security and rate limiting

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account, including email verification
  • To process purchases and author payouts
  • To deliver purchased Content securely (time-limited download links)
  • To send transactional emails (verification, password reset, purchase receipts, payout confirmations)
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access (e.g. rate limiting on login attempts)
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services We Use

ServicePurpose
PaystackPayment processing and author payouts
CloudinaryCover image and avatar hosting
Backblaze B2Secure storage of purchased content files
ResendDelivery of transactional emails
MongoDB AtlasDatabase hosting

Each of these providers processes data under their own privacy and security terms. We only share the minimum data necessary for them to perform their function (e.g. Paystack receives payment details directly; we do not store your card information).

4. Data Retention

We retain account and transaction data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we deactivate it and anonymize personally identifying fields (name, email, avatar), while retaining order and financial records as required for accounting, tax, and dispute-resolution purposes.

5. Your Rights

You can update your profile information, change your password, or delete your account at any time from account settings. Authors must withdraw any pending balance before deletion. To request a copy of your data or ask questions about how it's used, contact us at ivnabin11@gmail.com.

6. Security

Passwords are hashed and never stored in plaintext. Password-reset and email-verification tokens are hashed at rest. We use rate limiting on login, password reset, and verification endpoints to reduce the risk of brute-force attacks, and standard security headers across the Platform.

7. Children's Privacy

Yorha is not directed at individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. International Users

Yorha operates from Nigeria and primarily serves Nigerian users and creators. If you access the Platform from outside Nigeria, your information will be processed in Nigeria and by the third-party providers listed above, which may be located in other countries.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or a notice on the Platform.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to ivnabin11@gmail.com.
